Feature 1: Step-by-Step Action Items with Checkboxes

Clear, actionable steps students can check off as they progress

üéØ Learning the Mezzanine

‚è±Ô∏è 5 minutes
‚≠ê Easy

Follow these steps to explore the Mezzanine panel at the top of Roblox Studio. Check off each step as you complete it!

  • 1

    Open Roblox Studio

    Launch Roblox Studio from your desktop. If you don't have it installed, download it from create.roblox.com

  • 2

    Look at the very top of your screen

    The Mezzanine is the horizontal bar running across the top. You should see tabs like "Model", "Avatar", "Script", and "UI".

  • 3

    Click on different tabs

    Try clicking on the "Model" tab, then "Avatar" tab. Notice how the toolbar below changes to show different tools.

  • 4

    Find the playtest buttons

    On the left side of the Mezzanine, locate the green "Play" button and the "Pause" button. These let you test your game!

  • 5

    Check for the Assistant icon

    On the right side, you might see an "Assistant" icon that looks like a lightbulb or robot. This is your AI helper!

‚ö†Ô∏è Watch Out - Common Mistakes

  • Can't find the Mezzanine? Make sure you're in the main Studio window, not the startup screen
  • Tabs not changing? You might need to wait a second for Studio to load the tools
  • Playtest buttons disabled? You need to have a place/game open first
