#### Step 2: Understanding the Sustainability Story
**Instruction**: Read about why this game matters

**Content**:

Every year, millions of electric vehicle (EV) batteries reach the end of their useful life. But here's the cool part â€“ these batteries still have 70-80% of their capacity left! Instead of throwing them away, we can **upcycle** them into Energy Storage Systems (ESS).

**In Our Game**:
1. Players collect **used EV batteries**
2. Take them to the **Battery Conversion Center**
3. Complete mini-games to convert them into **1kWh battery blocks**
4. Transport blocks to the **E-Charger Center**
5. Power an epic **K-Pop concert**!

**Real-World Impact**:
- Reduces electronic waste
- Saves precious materials (Lithium, Nickel, Manganese)
- Provides clean energy storage
- Teaches sustainable thinking

**Fun Fact**: A 100-person K-Pop concert needs about 100 kWh per hour. If the concert lasts 2 hours, you need 200 kWh total. That's 200 of your 1kWh battery blocks!
